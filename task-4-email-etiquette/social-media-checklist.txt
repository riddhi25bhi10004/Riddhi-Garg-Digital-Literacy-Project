Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

DO’s 
- Think before you post and consider how it may affect your academic and professional reputation. 
- Use respectful and polite language in comments, messages, and discussions. 
- Protect your privacy by adjusting security settings and limiting personal information shared publicly. 
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation. 
- Maintain a professional profile if connecting with professors, recruiters, or internship coordinators.

DON’Ts 
- Do not post offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content. 
- Do not share confidential academic or organizational information online. 
- Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying. 
- Do not post impulsively when angry or emotional. 
- Do not plagiarize content or share copyrighted material without permission.
